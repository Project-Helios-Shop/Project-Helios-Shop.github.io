/// 04 — Energy ///

The Dyson Swarm

The human fleet draws its power from an unprecedented technology: the Dyson Swarm. Billions of independent collector satellites orbit encountered stars, capturing their radiation and redistributing energy to each ship via directional microwave beams.

Each ship can deploy its own autonomous mini-satellites to maximize its energy harvest, or connect to the collective Swarm network in exchange for shared computational resources.

The system is inherently scalable: every element is independent, added or removed without affecting the whole. A galactic infrastructure that grows with humanity — indefinitely.
